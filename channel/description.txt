Welcome to Dangerous eBikers

Timestamped footage of e-bikes ridden illegally on UK pavements and footpaths. Awareness and evidence for safer streets — not naming, shaming, or harassing riders.

Awareness • Evidence • Change

PRIVACY & FOOTAGE
• Originals kept private; published clips are anonymised (faces blurred, metadata stripped)
• Uploads are private until reviewed, then made public
• Removal requests: ajlennon@gmail.com

COMPLIANCE (public)

GUIDELINES
• Factual evidence only — no doxxing or abuse
• No confrontation or vigilantism
• Report danger: 999 · Non-emergency: 101
• This channel does not replace a formal police report

Documenting eBike danger. For safer streets in the UK.

Welcome to Dangerous eBikers

Timestamped, geotagged footage of e-bikes ridden illegally on UK pavements and footpaths. Awareness and evidence for safer streets — not naming, shaming, or harassing riders.

Awareness • Evidence • Change

PRIVACY & FOOTAGE
• Original recordings are kept private and are not uploaded to YouTube
• Published clips are anonymised: faces blurred before upload
• Embedded GPS/device metadata is removed from published files
• Time and location may appear in video descriptions for incident logging only
• Unlisted uploads where possible; originals retained for police reports (101)
• If you appear in footage and want it removed, contact the channel

GUIDELINES
• Factual evidence only — no doxxing or abuse in comments
• Do not encourage confrontation or vigilantism
• Report danger: 999. Non-emergency: 101
• This channel does not replace a formal police report

Documenting eBike danger. For safer streets in the UK.
